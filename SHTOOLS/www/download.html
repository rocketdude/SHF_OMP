To download SHTOOLS, click on one of the following files:

SHTOOLS2.8.zip - Unix zipped directory (2 Mb)

To uncompress this file, type

unzip SHTOOLS2.8.zip

SHTOOLS2.8.tar.Z - Unix tarred and compressed directory (2 Mb)

To uncompress this file, type

uncompress SHTOOLS2.8.tar.Z
tar -xf SHTOOLS2.8.tar
