YilmIndex

       YilmIndex - Compute the index of an 1D array of spherical harmonic
                   coefficients corresponding to I, L, and M.

SYNOPSIS

       INTEGER FUNCTION YilmIndex ( I, L, M )

           INTEGER   I, L, M

DESCRIPTION

       YilmIndex will calculate the index of a 1D vector of spherical harmonic
       coefficients corresponding to degree L, angular order M and I (1 =
       cosine, 2 = sine). The index is given by 1 + L**2 + (I-1)*L + M.

ARGUMENTS

       I         (input) INTEGER

                 1 corresponds to the Cosine coefficient, and 2 corresponds to
                 the Sine coefficient.

       L         (input) INTEGER

                 The spherical harmonic degree.

       M         (input) INTEGER

                 The angular order.
